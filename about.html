ABOUT EDUGENTECH
OUR MISSION

Edugentech academy mission is to create user-friendly technology courses that teach and impact young adults from various minority backgrounds and underserved communities, providing much needed exposure and awareness at a young age to motivate and inspire academic and professional pathways.

OUR VISSION

Our vission is to embolden underrepresnted communities with the essential technological skills to strive for academic pathways in technology-related fields that will diversify the digital workforce and economy.
