ABOUT EDUGENTECH
OUR MISSION

EDUGENTECH mission is to provide the most innovative energy solutions for our customers by our expert technical team in executing and operating the projects efficiently. We are working to maintain the highest standards of our quality, safety and environmental sustainability.

OUR VISSION

Our vission is to Empower Nigeria and creating a sustainable future through solar Energy. We desire to fufill 100% of our energy needs with renewable energy and to provide the most compelling value in solar industry with cost effective sustainable energy solutions across all the markets to make pollution free Nigeria and improve the quality of present and future generations.
